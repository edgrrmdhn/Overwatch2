A SWEET HOLIDAY SURPRISE! INTRODUCING LEGENDARY
GINGERBREAD BASTION

Edgar Ramadhani | December 20, 2022

The heroes of Overwatch are dashing through the snow in this year’s Winter Wonderland! This event features a special delivery of new arcade modes, cosmetics, free rewards, and plenty of good times to be had with teammates and friends!

We’re also cooking up a sweet holiday treat just for you! Starting now through January 2, you can buy the all-new Gingerbread Legendary Bastion skin for a single Overwatch Coin.

Even if you are brand new to Overwatch 2 and don’t have any Overwatch Coins yet, we have you covered. Completing weekly challenges in the game will award Overwatch Coins for free, and thanks to our friends at Microsoft, you can now redeem Microsoft Reward Points for Overwatch Coins. Log into Overwatch 2 now through January 2 to get a special gift from the team. Just by opening the game, players will receive the Festive Wreath weapon charm and the 2023 player icon. Stick around and complete special, holiday challenges to earn even more free rewards—including the Ice Queen Epic Brigitte skin.

Just remember, the offer to get the Legendary skin and free login rewards ends on January 2, so don’t let the cookie crumble!

The Winter Wonderland free login gift, seasonal challenges, and free rewards will be available until January 3.

From all of us here at Blizzard, we want to say thank you to the millions of players who have joined us already. We wish you a very wonderful holiday week and can’t wait to show you what we have in store for the new year.

Happy Holidays!
